General Questions

Orders & Delivery

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website by selecting your desired items and adding them to your cart. We also accept phone orders at (555) 123-4567. For large orders, we recommend calling us at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability.

What are your delivery hours?

We offer delivery Monday through Saturday from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Sunday deliveries are available from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Same-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2:00 PM, subject to availability.

Is there a minimum order for delivery?

Yes, we have a minimum order of $25 for delivery within a 5-mile radius. For deliveries beyond 5 miles, the minimum is $40. Free delivery is available for orders over $75.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup or delivery time. Please contact us immediately if you need to make changes. Orders within 24 hours cannot be cancelled, but we may be able to make minor modifications.

Products & Ingredients

Are your products made fresh daily?

Absolutely! All our breads, pastries, and cakes are baked fresh every morning starting at 4:00 AM. We never sell day-old products, and any unsold items are donated to local charities at the end of each day.

Do you use organic ingredients?

We use organic flour and butter in all our premium products. Our eggs are free-range, and we source seasonal fruits from local organic farms whenever possible. Look for the "Organic" label on our product descriptions.

How long do your products stay fresh?

Our breads stay fresh for 2-3 days when stored in a paper bag at room temperature. Pastries are best enjoyed within 24 hours. Cakes can be refrigerated for up to 5 days. We recommend freezing items if you need to store them longer.

Can I request custom cakes or special orders?

Yes! We love creating custom cakes for special occasions. Please give us at least 1 week notice for custom cake orders. Contact us to discuss your design ideas, flavors, and dietary requirements. We also offer custom cookie platters and party boxes.

Payment & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, cash, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For online orders, you can also pay via PayPal. Corporate accounts can be set up for regular bulk orders.

Do you offer discounts or loyalty programs?

Yes! Join our Baeery Rewards program to earn 1 point for every dollar spent. Redeem points for free items and exclusive discounts. We also offer 10% off for bulk orders over $100 and special promotions throughout the year.

What is your refund policy?

Customer satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, please contact us within 24 hours and we'll gladly provide a refund or replacement. Custom orders require 50% deposit which is non-refundable.

Allergens & Dietary Options

Do you have gluten-free options?

Yes! We offer a variety of gluten-free breads, muffins, and cakes made in a dedicated gluten-free area to prevent cross-contamination. Look for items marked with our GF label. Please note that we cannot guarantee 100% gluten-free environment.

Do you have vegan options?

Absolutely! Our vegan selection includes several bread varieties, cookies, and select pastries. We can also create custom vegan cakes with advance notice. All vegan items are clearly labeled in our display cases.

How do you handle food allergies?

We take allergies very seriously. All our products are labeled with allergen information including nuts, dairy, eggs, and soy. Please inform our staff of any allergies when ordering, and we'll help you find safe options. We maintain detailed ingredient lists for all items.

Are your products suitable for diabetics?

We offer several low-sugar and sugar-free options sweetened with natural alternatives. Nutritional information is available for all products. We recommend consulting with your healthcare provider about incorporating our products into your diet plan.

Still have questions?

Can't find the answer you're looking for? Our friendly team is here to help.
